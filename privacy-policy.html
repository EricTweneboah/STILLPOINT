4. Legal basis for processing

Under UK data protection law, we rely on:

  • Contract: to deliver agreed coaching services

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers only when needed for booking, payment, communications, or legal compliance.

6. Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for service delivery, legal requirements, and legitimate business needs. Coaching notes are retained securely for continuity and safeguarding purposes.

7. Data security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

8. Your rights

You may have the right to:
